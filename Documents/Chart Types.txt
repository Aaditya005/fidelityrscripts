
	Chart Type	-	Purpose

	Pie Chart	-	Showing Proportions of a data point to the Whole

	Line Chart	-	Showing Trends of a metric over time
					(Time series)

	Dual Line Chart	-	Adding Multiple Metrics side-by-side

	Bar Chart	-	Comparing a metric across categories

	Histogram	-	Showing distribution of a data

	Scatterplot	-	Showing relationship of multiple variables

	Bullet Graph	-	Showing Performance of a metric again goal

	Gantt Bar	-	Showing things in use over time

	Heat Map	-	Showing how variables are concentrated and finding hot-spots

	Box & Whisker
	(Boxplot)	-	Showing distribution of set of data
